Discover Saba

The Unspoiled Queen

Saba is a 5 km² volcanic island in the Caribbean Netherlands — a special municipality of the Netherlands. Rising dramatically from the sea, it's crowned by Mount Scenery, the highest point in the Kingdom of the Netherlands.

With no beaches but spectacular diving, world-class hiking, and authentic Caribbean charm, Saba attracts those seeking something different. The island's four villages — The Bottom (the capital), Windwardside, St. Johns, and Zion's Hill — are connected by "The Road," an engineering marvel carved into the volcanic rock.

Property here is limited and precious, making it an ideal destination for those seeking privacy, natural beauty, and a genuine community connection.
